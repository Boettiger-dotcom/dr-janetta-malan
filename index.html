About Dr. Malan

The Approach

As a general practitioner, Dr. Malan provides patient-focused care by diagnosing and treating common illnesses, managing chronic conditions, conducting routine check-ups, and referring patients to specialists when necessary.

General Medical Consultations

Consultations for adults and children covering acute illness, injuries, and general health concerns.

Chronic Condition Management

Ongoing care for conditions such as diabetes and hypertension, with regular monitoring and tailored treatment plans.

Preventative Healthcare

Wellness checks, health screenings, and basic medical procedures to keep you and your family in good health.
